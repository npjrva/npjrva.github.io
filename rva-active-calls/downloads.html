Data description

These dumps are provided as gzip-compressed CSV files. The columns are described below. Columns 1--7 are copied verbatim from the official active calls website. Columns 8 and 9 represent a best-effort geolocation for the provided location. Caution: geolocation is imperfect and contains both omissions and errors.

  1. time_received - YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM timestamp
  2. agency
  3. dispatch_area
  4. unit
  5. call_type
  6. location
  7. status
  8. latitude - Latitude of the location field, or empty.
  9. longitude - Longitude of the location field, or empty.
